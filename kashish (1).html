My dearest Kashish,

There are a thousand things I want to say to you and never enough words to say them right. So let me try, imperfectly but sincerely.

You walked into my life and made it brighter — not in a flashy, loud way, but in that quiet, steady way that matters most. The way sunlight fills a room without asking permission.

You are my favourite kind of wonderful. The kind that gets better the more I know you. The kind that makes ordinary days feel like something worth remembering.

I love the way you are — your strength, your softness, your laugh that I'd travel any distance to hear. I love that you are exactly you, and that you let me be here beside you.

Thank you for every moment. For the good ones, the hard ones, and all the ones in between. I wouldn't trade a single one of them.

You deserve every beautiful thing — and I hope, every single day, to be a small part of that.

Forever yours, with all my heart. 🌹
